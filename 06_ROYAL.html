MOTIVATION

This is an installation work for Royal Salute rebranding.
Royal Salute, which represents the luxurious image of 'Queen's Alcohol', has not taken any promotional methods other than the existing advanced strategy for a long time. We assumed that the brand 'Royal Salute' made its own complex cultural place called the 'Royal Salute House'. The approach of our work might be fresh for this brand because they usually take the more traditional way for the promotion which is emphasizing the high-class images.
Based on this fact, what if top brands such as Royal Salute move away from the cliché and move on to a new promotional strategy for the times? We planned this project using imagination.

This work is for the section on the 1st floor of the 'Royal Salute House' which is planned for the temporary exhibitions.
