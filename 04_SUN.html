MOTIVATION

From the Sun is a mobile healing game presented to modern people living with electronic devices such as smartphones and tablets. It's a unique type of healing game that makes modern people who are used to the videos think about what kind of situation they are in.

It has become a daily routine for modern people to access social media, webtoons, content platforms, and news more than once a day as a habit, even if there is nothing in particular to see. Also, with the development of the media, various kinds of videos and images can be seen, so people can easily enjoy the contents anytime, anywhere, regardless of their given time. This game is based on these modern habits and the story of this game goes on simply by accessing it, just like watching other video contents.



CONCEPT OF THE GAME

The concept of the game is motivated from a poem From the Sun to the Boy (1908) written by Nam-sun Choi. The story of the game begins as the lonely sun talks to the boy. You can build affection through conversation with the sun, bring animal or plant friends, or present natural objects and buildings to the sky(sun).
