ABOUT CRP(Creative Platform Service)
& PROJECT

'CRP' is a social network platform service operated by a 'SOOM Foundation', which connect creators, producers, and investors organically.

This project was for SNS promotion of CRP service and includes two short videos and one moving poster.



CONCEPT

The universe, concept of the CRP service, is base of this work. Two short promotion videos are based on a slogan "You can experience new world with CRP".

> First film, "CRP DOOR" : "Through the CRP, you can take the world as a whole."
> Second film, "CRP TELESCOPE" : "Various contents can be accessed through the CRP."
> Moving poster : Graphic based on the concept, CRP universe.
