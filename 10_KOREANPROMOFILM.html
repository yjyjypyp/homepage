MOTIVATION

It is a Korean promotional film to promote original Korean words, which are not well known.
The character and the story was composed based on three selected words.

  Selected words :
   . 꽃멀미 (Kot-mul-mi) : Dizzy symptoms caused by the beauty and fragrance of flowers
   . 비거스렁이 (Bi-geo-su-rung-e): A phenomenon in which wind blows and temperatures drop
    after the rain clears up.
   . 그루잠 (Gru-jam): Falling asleep again after being awaken



STORY

Daeun(26) who has her first job falls asleep with tension and anxiety, but couldn't sleep well because of her dream and rain.

The main character is about to go to work the next morning. It's going to be the first day. It's raining outside.
The main character woke up and went back to sleep, but when she saw countless flowers in her dream and felt flower sickness, the flowers died and suddenly the dream changed negatively. She wakes up; the had rain stopped and a cold wind blew.
