MOTIVATION

It is an illustration work that expresses an exciting personality('Heung' in Korean) using hwatu(gambling card game which originated in Japan).

I was often told that I had a lot of excitement, so I tried to express my character "heung." The concept conjured up 'Play' and expressed it in conjunction with 'Hwatu'. The illustration was composed to make the elements in the painting of the Hwatu's card and to designed the movement of the card itself feel alive.
