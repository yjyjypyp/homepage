WORK

Like a Pure Spirit is an experimental video and book design work that compares several manipulation scandals in Korea with the characteristics of "Joseon Film", the first movie which was brought to Korea by Japanese people. The reality of  "Joseon Film", controlled by external power but not known to the public,  seemed not very different from the contemporary period. Based on the record about "Joseon Film", video is methodologically connected to how our societies are controlled by an inevitable force.

- scenes -


PROCESS

More than 600 papers were scanned to make scenes.
After making the images and letting them move, I rendered the whole scene as image files with 12 frames.
Then, all these scenes were scanned using rubbing to the scanner.  This process created unpredictable new images.
Additionally, card type magnifying glasses were used to make distortion during display, so that the audience won't see it as they want.

BOOK : research and production book


The book includes researches about Joseon Film and production process of 'Like a Pure Spirit'(2020).
This book consists of A-B-A' format.

A : flipping book (printed on semi-transparent paper)
B : research and production book
A' : flipping book
